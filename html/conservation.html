PROTECT OUR OCEANS

MAJOR THREATS

Overfishing

Depleting fish populations faster than they can reproduce.

Pollution

Plastic waste, oil spills, and agricultural runoff harming marine life.

Climate Change

Ocean warming, acidification, and coral bleaching.

HOW TO HELP

1. Reduce single-use plastics.
2. Support sustainable seafood.
3. Respect marine ecosystems.
4. Educate others about conservation.
